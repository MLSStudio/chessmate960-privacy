Chess960: Online Chess

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ChessMate 960 collects, uses, and stores data when you use the app.

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use ChessMate 960, we may collect:

  • Account information such as username, email address, avatar, and profile photo
  • Gameplay information such as moves, match results, ratings, time controls, and game history
  • Live feature data such as chat messages and online presence timestamps
  • Technical information needed to keep the app stable and secure

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with opponents and sync live games
  • To display your profile, ratings, and match history
  • To enable in-game chat and friend play features
  • To improve performance, security, and reliability

3. Services We Use

ChessMate 960 uses Firebase services for authentication, data storage, and real-time gameplay syncing. If advertising is enabled in the future, additional providers may collect data according to their own policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Some information is processed by trusted service providers required to run the app, such as Firebase infrastructure used for account and gameplay services.

5. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion inside the app from Settings > Delete Account. If deletion fails because your sign-in is no longer recent, sign in again and retry.
